General Rules

Please follow these rules during our cultural preservation events:

  • 1. Respect all participants and staff at all times.
  • 2. Follow the event schedule as closely as possible.
  • 3. No disruptive behavior or harassment will be tolerated.
  • 4. Please wear appropriate cultural attire during activities.
  • 5. Stay hydrated and take breaks as needed.

Cultural Guidelines

Our events celebrate Thai culture. Please follow these guidelines:

  • 1. Participate in cultural activities with an open mind and respect.
  • 2. Avoid using mobile devices during performances.
  • 3. Be mindful of traditional Thai values.
  • 4. Engage with others respectfully.

Safety Protocols

To ensure safety during the event, please follow these protocols:

  • 1. Report any emergencies to event staff immediately.
  • 2. Follow instructions regarding safety and logistics.
  • 3. Stay aware of your surroundings.
  • 4. Follow local health guidelines.
